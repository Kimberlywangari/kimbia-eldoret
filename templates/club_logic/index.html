About Us

We are a vibrant community of runners and walkers in Eldoret dedicated to celebrating holistic health, lifelong friendships, and the shared love of the open road. Our aim is to transform our city into a space where every stride strengthens both our physical well-being and our social bonds. Whether you are chasing a new personal best or enjoying a steady Saturday stroll, there is a place for you in our tribe.

Rules

  • Pets must be on a leash.
  • Children under 14 must be accompanied by an adult.

Our Mission

To build community by making movement accessible, enjoyable and inclusive.

Our Vision

A city where running and walking together strengthens health and relationships.
